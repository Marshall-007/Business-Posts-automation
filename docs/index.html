Connection

Connect this dashboard to the company's GitHub repository. Everything else needs this first.

How this works
  • The dashboard is a plain web page: it talks directly to the GitHub API with the token you paste here. There is no other server.
  • The token needs Actions: read/write and Contents: read/write on this one repository only.
  • Managing several companies? Use the Admin portal (top right) to save each company and jump between their dashboards.

Stored only in this browser (localStorage). It is never sent anywhere except GitHub's API.

Schedule one-off posts

Hand-pick up to three images or videos and give each a day and time. Good for one-off promos; use Content campaigns below for hands-free monthly posting.

How this works
  • Each file is sized automatically (feed 1080x1080, story 1080x1920), uploaded to the repo, posted at its chosen time, then deleted.
  • Feed videos post as Reels; story videos post as Stories (MP4/MOV, under 40 MB). Stories vanish after 24 hours and ignore the caption.
  • Everything also mirrors to Facebook (and feed items to TikTok) when those platforms are connected.
  • The Queue below lists everything waiting to post, from this section and from campaigns; pending items can be cancelled.

Content campaigns (hands-free posting)

Hands-free posting, one month at a time: tick a month's checkbox, give it a start date, choose its platforms, and it posts itself day by day.

How this works
  • A campaign is a folder under content/. Inside it, months (Month 1, Month 2...) hold day folders, and each day holds a Post and a Story folder of images/videos.
  • Each month is a batch with its own checkbox, start date, and platform selection: only checked months post, day 1 on the month's start date, day 2 the next day, and so on.
  • Within one day: 2 files go out 6 hours apart, 3 files 4 hours apart, 4+ files 3 hours apart, starting at the posts/stories times set above the months.
  • Captions: a photo1.txt next to photo1.jpg, or one caption.txt per folder, else a unique randomized caption with brand, niche and viral hashtags plus your contact details is written for every post. Stories have no caption.
  • Images are converted and sized automatically (.webp/.png are fine); each file is deleted from the repo after it posts.
  • Platform icons on each month show where it posts: Instagram, Facebook (feed + stories), TikTok (feed only; TikTok has no stories API).
  • Safety: months whose dates are long past are not flooded out, and at most 20 posts go out per 24 hours.

Bulk upload and auto-sort

Drop a pile of unsorted photos here and they are sorted into a month of day folders, ready to schedule.

How this works
  • Pick the campaign and the month to fill: an existing month continues its own day numbering; a new month (e.g. Month 2) is created starting at Day 1.
  • Feed images fill each day's Post folder (2 or 3 per day) and story images the Story folder; everything is sized and committed in one go.
  • New campaigns and months are registered automatically, unchecked, so nothing posts until you set the month's date, tick it, and Save.

Flows

How this works

A flow is a reusable preset (for example "Weekly promo" with a set caption and image). Run one from here any time; it publishes through the same workflow as everything else. Flows live in data/flows.json.
