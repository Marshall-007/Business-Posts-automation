Schedule Instagram posts

Add up to three images or videos with a day/time for each, and choose Feed or Story. Media is uploaded to your repo, posted at the chosen time, then removed. Feed images are squared to 1080x1080; Story images are sized to 1080x1920. Feed videos post as Reels; Story videos post as Stories (MP4/MOV, under 40 MB). Stories vanish after 24 hours and ignore the caption. Requires the repo to be Public.

Content folders (hands-free posting)

Drop images or videos into folders in the repo and they post automatically, no uploading through this page. Structure: content/day1/posts and content/day1/stories, then day2, day3, and so on. day1 posts on the start date below. If a folder holds 2 files they go out 6 hours apart; 3 files, 4 hours apart (the first at the start time). Images are converted and sized automatically (.webp and .png are fine). Captions: add photo1.txt next to photo1.jpg, or one caption.txt for the folder; otherwise a caption is written for you. Files are removed after posting. Queued items appear in the Queue above, where they can be cancelled.

Open the content folders on GitHub to add files (open a day folder, then Add file, then Upload files).
